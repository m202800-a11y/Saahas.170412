UPI Facts 99% Indians Don’t Know!

Discover surprising, hidden and amazing facts about India’s Unified Payments Interface you’ve never heard before.

💡
UPI Works 24x7, Even on Holidays!
While NEFT & RTGS have banking hours, UPI never sleeps — you can send money 24/7, 365 days a year, including public holidays.
🌍
UPI Goes Global
UPI is now accepted in countries like Singapore, UAE, Nepal, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, and France, making cross-border payments easier for Indians.
🔒
UPI Is More Secure Than Card Payments
UPI uses two-factor authentication (your device + UPI PIN), so your card and account details are never shared with merchants.
🚀
UPI Handles Over 10 Billion Transactions Monthly
As of 2024, UPI processes more transactions every month than all credit & debit cards in India combined.
🆓
No Charges for Most UPI Transfers
Sending money via UPI between banks is free for most users. Even merchants often pay zero MDR (Merchant Discount Rate) for UPI Lite.
👨‍💻
UPI Is Open to All — No App Restriction
You can use any UPI-enabled app (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, etc.) to pay any UPI address — it’s fully interoperable!
⚡
Instant Refunds
Failed UPI transactions are usually reversed instantly or within minutes; no more waiting for days for your refund.
🔗
UPI Can Be Linked to Credit Cards
You can now link RuPay credit cards with UPI and pay at shops using your credit limit, not just your bank balance.
🎁
Send Gifts via UPI
Some UPI apps allow you to send money as a gift with custom messages and wrapping — perfect for birthdays or festivals!
🕵️‍♂️
UPI IDs Hide Your Bank Details
When you pay with UPI, your bank account number is never revealed — only your UPI ID is shared.
